Act as a senior SaaS product architect, AI workflow designer, UX designer, and full-stack Next.js developer.

I am building GetQuotePro.ai, a SaaS product for cleaning businesses that helps users calculate pricing, generate professional quotes, and manage leads.

I want you to design and implement a major feature called:

Smart Quote Intake

CORE PRODUCT GOAL

This feature should solve a real problem for cleaning businesses:

Customers often prefer texting or asynchronous communication instead of phone calls, but this creates messy, incomplete conversations that make quoting slow, inconsistent, and sloppy.

Smart Quote Intake should allow a cleaning business to collect all the information needed for a quote in a structured, fast, user-friendly way.

IMPORTANT PRODUCT RULE

The AI must NOT invent pricing.
The final price must always follow the pricing settings configured by the business owner inside the app.

That means the architecture must be:

1. Business owner configures pricing rules in the app
2. Customer submits job details through guided intake or AI-assisted intake
3. AI can help collect and structure information
4. The pricing engine applies the business owner’s configured rules
5. The system generates a quote draft
6. The business owner can review, adjust if needed, and send

The AI should assist with intake and data extraction, but not override pricing rules.
