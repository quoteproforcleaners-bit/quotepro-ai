SPRINT 20B — Automated Cleaner Job 
Notification System

Build an automated system that notifies 
cleaners (team members) about their upcoming 
jobs via email AND SMS. This feature should 
be configured during onboarding AND 
accessible from settings.

This is a separate onboarding step from 
Sprint 20A — it comes immediately AFTER 
the customer reminder step.

═══════════════════════════════
BACKEND — CLEANER NOTIFICATIONS
═══════════════════════════════

1. ADD to user settings:
   cleanerNotificationsEnabled: boolean
   cleanerNotificationDays: integer (0 = same day, 1 = day before)
   cleanerNotificationEmail: boolean (default true)
   cleanerNotificationSms: boolean (default true)

2. SAVE onboarding selection:
   POST /api/user/cleaner-notification-preferences
   Body: { enabled: boolean }

3. UPDATE JOB ASSIGNMENT FLOW:
   When a job is assigned to a team member 
   AND cleanerNotificationsEnabled = true:
   
   Immediately send assignment notification:
   
   EMAIL to team member:
     Subject: "New job assigned: 
     [ClientName] on [Date]"
     
     Body:
     "Hi [CleanerFirstName],
     
     You have a cleaning job assigned:
     
     CLIENT: [ClientName]
     ADDRESS: [JobAddress]
     DATE: [DayOfWeek], [Date]
     TIME: [StartTime] - [EndTime]
     SERVICE: [JobType]
     NOTES: [InternalNotes]
     
     [If pets] ⚠️ Pets: [PetInfo]
     [If access] 🔑 Access: [AccessNotes]
     
     Please confirm you've seen this 
     by replying to this email.
     
     [BusinessName]"
   
   SMS to team member:
     "New job: [ClientFirstName] at 
     [Address] on [Day] at [Time]. 
     [ServiceType]. Check email for details."

4. REMINDER SCHEDULER for cleaners:
   
   Separate from customer reminders.
   Runs every hour.
   
   Find jobs where:
     startDatetime = tomorrow (within 24h)
     assignedTeamMemberId is not null
     cleanerNotificationsEnabled = true
     cleaner reminder not already sent
   
   Send REMINDER email to cleaner:
     Subject: "Reminder: Job tomorrow — 
     [ClientName] at [Time]"
     
     Body:
     "Hi [CleanerFirstName],
     
     Reminder about tomorrow's job:
     
     CLIENT: [ClientName]
     ADDRESS: [JobAddress]
     TIME: [StartTime]
     SERVICE: [JobType]
     
     CHECKLIST ITEMS: [if any]
     [bullet list of checklist items]
     
     SPECIAL NOTES: [InternalNotes]
     
     See you tomorrow!
     [BusinessName]"
   
   SMS reminder to cleaner:
     "Reminder: [ClientFirstName] tomorrow 
     at [Time], [Address]. 
     [ServiceType]. Reply STOP to opt out."

5. WORK ORDER EMAIL:
   Business owner can manually trigger 
   a work order send from the job detail screen.
   
   This is different from the automatic reminder —
   it's a detailed work order PDF attachment.
   
   Work Order PDF content:
     Header: [BusinessName] logo + "WORK ORDER"
     Job #: [ID]
     Date: [Date]
     Time: [StartTime] - [EndTime]
     Assigned to: [CleanerName]
     
     CLIENT INFORMATION:
     Name: [ClientName]
     Address: [JobAddress]
     Phone: [ClientPhone]
     
     SERVICE DETAILS:
     Type: [JobType]
     Frequency: [Recurrence]
     Notes: [InternalNotes]
     
     CHECKLIST:
     [All checklist items with empty checkboxes]
     
     ACCESS INFORMATION:
     [If key code / lockbox / etc]
     
     Footer: "Please complete all checklist 
     items and note any issues."
   
   Generate this PDF server-side using 
   existing PDF generation library.
   Attach to the work order email.

6. ADD reminder_log entries for cleaner 
   notifications (same table, different type):
   reminder_type: "cleaner_assignment" | 
   "cleaner_reminder" | "work_order"

═══════════════════════════════
JOB DETAIL SCREEN — SEND WORK ORDER
═══════════════════════════════

On the Job Detail screen, add a 
"Send Work Order" button:

Position: in the Overview tab, below 
the job details card

Button style:
  Outlined, secondary
  Icon: Feather "send"
  Label: "Send Work Order to Cleaner"

On tap:
  If no cleaner assigned: 
    Show alert "Assign a cleaner first"
  If cleaner has no email:
    Show alert "Add cleaner email in 
    Team Members settings"
  Otherwise:
    Generate work order PDF
    Send email with PDF attached
    Show success toast: 
    "Work order sent to [CleanerName]"
    Log in reminder_log

SETTINGS PAGE
═══════════════════════════════

Add "Team Notifications" section to Settings:

Title: "Cleaner Job Notifications"
Subtitle: "Automatically notify your 
team about upcoming jobs"

MASTER TOGGLE:
  "Notify cleaners automatically"
  Switch: on/off

When on, show:
  EMAIL sub-toggle: 
    "Send email notifications" on/off
  
  SMS sub-toggle:
    "Send text notifications" on/off
  
  TIMING dropdown:
    "Notify cleaners:"
    Options:
      "When job is assigned (immediately)"
      "Day before the job"
      "Both — when assigned AND day before"
      ← Default: "Both"

TEAM MEMBER LIST:
  Show each active team member with:
  Name | Phone | Email | Notifications toggle
  
  Individual toggle per team member:
    Allows disabling notifications for 
    specific cleaners (e.g. owner/admin)
  
  "Add email" link for members missing email

TEST BUTTON per team member:
  "Send test notification →"
  Sends a sample job notification to 
  that specific team member

  order emails and job notifications"

  warning in the Team Member list:
  "⚠️ No email — SMS only"
