SPRINT 22 — Dashboard Simplification
"Built for Cleaning Business Owners, Not Analysts"

Redesign the QuotePro dashboard to be 
radically simpler and more actionable for 
residential cleaning business owners. 

These users are not enterprise operators. 
They are small business owners — many of 
whom are also the cleaner. They open the 
app between jobs, on their phone, with 
dirty gloves. Every element must earn 
its place.

DESIGN PRINCIPLE FOR THIS SPRINT:
"If a cleaning business owner can't act 
on it in the next 5 minutes, it doesn't 
belong on the home screen."

═══════════════════════════════
REMOVE THESE ENTIRELY
═══════════════════════════════

1. The subtitle "Your revenue operations 
   command center" — delete it. 
   Replace with nothing or with 
   "[X] jobs this week · $[X] earned"
   which is actually useful.

2. The "QuotePro is fully set up" 
   green card — remove from dashboard.
   It belongs in onboarding only.
   Once setup is complete, this card 
   should never appear again.

3. The four duplicate stat cards 
   (Revenue Won, Active Jobs, Close Rate, 
   Pipeline Value) — these repeat what's 
   already in the hero strip above.
   Remove the cards. Keep the hero strip.

4. The hero strip labels need plain language:
   CURRENT: "TODAY'S REVENUE" → KEEP
   CURRENT: "JOBS THIS WEEK" → KEEP
   CURRENT: "CLOSE RATE" → CHANGE TO 
     "QUOTES WON" (owners understand this)
   CURRENT: "FOLLOW-UPS DUE TODAY" → 
     CHANGE TO "NEED FOLLOW-UP"

5. "GROWTH" nav section label → 
   Change to "BUSINESS"
   
6. Remove from nav entirely:
   - "Lead Radar" (move to Lead Capture submenu)
   - "Weekly Recap" (move to Revenue page)
   - "Reactivation" (move to Opportunities)
   This brings the nav from 15+ items 
   to under 10 visible at a time.

═══════════════════════════════
REDESIGN THE HERO SECTION
═══════════════════════════════

CURRENT hero is too corporate. 
Make it warm and personal:

GREETING:
  "Good [morning/afternoon/evening],"
  "[BusinessName]" (bold, large)
  
  Below: dynamic context line —
  If jobs today: "You have [X] job[s] today"
  If no jobs: "No jobs scheduled today"
  If revenue today > 0: 
    "· $[X] earned so far"
  
  Example: 
  "Good afternoon, Pristine Home Cleaning
   You have 2 jobs today · $366 earned so far"

STAT STRIP — keep but relabel:
  Keep the 4 stats but use plain language:
  
  1. TODAY'S REVENUE — keep as-is
     If $0: show "None yet today" not "$0"
  
  2. JOBS THIS WEEK — keep as-is
  
  3. QUOTES WON — (was Close Rate)
     Show as "[X] of [Y] quotes" 
     not a percentage
     e.g. "7 of 12 quotes"
     Percentage is meaningless to most 
     cleaning owners — won/total is clear
  
  4. NEED FOLLOW-UP — (was Follow-ups Due)
     Keep the count
     Color: amber if >0, green if 0

URGENCY BANNER (keep — this is good):
  "⚠️ 10 quotes need follow-up · 
  $3,713 at risk · Oldest 8d"
  "Act now →"
  
  This is the best element on the page.
  Keep it exactly as-is. Just make sure 
  it only shows when there ARE quotes 
  needing follow-up.

═══════════════════════════════
REPLACE THE 4 CARDS WITH 
"WHAT TO DO RIGHT NOW"
═══════════════════════════════

The space currently used by the 4 stat 
cards should become an action list:

SECTION TITLE: "Today's Focus"
(replace the cards entirely)

Show a maximum of 3-5 items, each 
with a one-tap action:

ITEM FORMAT:
  Left: priority indicator dot 
    (red = overdue, amber = due today, 
    blue = upcoming)
  Center: 
    Top line: customer name + action
    Bottom line: context detail
  Right: action button

EXAMPLE ITEMS:

🔴 "Call Jennifer Walsh"
    "Quote sent 8 days ago — $195 at risk"
    [Follow Up →]

🔴 "Follow up with Berwyn Athletic Club"  
    "Quote sent 2 days ago — $510 at risk"
    [Follow Up →]

🟡 "Schedule Sarah Mitchell"
    "Accepted quote — needs a date"
    [Schedule →]

🟡 "Request review from Patricia Nguyen"
    "Job completed 3 days ago"
    [Request →]

🔵 "Maria Santos — job tomorrow 10am"
    "Sarah Mitchell · 504 Chestnut Hill Rd"
    [View Job →]

GENERATION LOGIC:
Priority order:
  1. Overdue follow-ups (red)
  2. Accepted quotes needing scheduling (amber)
  3. Review requests ready to send (amber)
  4. Jobs tomorrow (blue)
  5. Upcoming jobs this week (blue)

Show maximum 5 items.
"View all →" link at the bottom.

If nothing to do:
  Single row with green checkmark:
  "You're all caught up! 
  Nothing urgent right now."

═══════════════════════════════

The current TODAY section is good 
but needs to be promoted higher on 
the page (currently buried) and made

MOVE IT: Put TODAY section directly 
after "Today's Focus" — before anything else.

REDESIGN the 4 mini-stats in TODAY:
  Current: Cleans Today | Revenue Today | 
           Next Clean | Needs Scheduling

  Problem: "Needs Scheduling: 13" is 
  a count with no names — useless.

  REPLACE WITH:

  Card 1: TODAY'S JOBS
    "[X] jobs today · [X] completed"

═══════════════════════════════
WHAT THE DASHBOARD SHOULD FEEL LIKE
═══════════════════════════════

After this sprint, a cleaning business 
owner should be able to open the app 
and within 5 seconds know:

1. Do I have jobs today? (hero + TODAY)
2. Who do I need to call? (Today's Focus)
3. Am I making money? (hero strip)

That's it. If they want more detail, 
they tap into the relevant section.

The dashboard is a DISPATCHER not 
a REPORTING TOOL. It tells you what 
to do next, not what happened last month.
