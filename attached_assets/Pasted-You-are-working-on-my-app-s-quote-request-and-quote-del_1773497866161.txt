- The total must be calculated from the actual quote data at send time and persisted correctly.
- The “Total” field and the “Accept Quote — $X.XX” button must always reflect the same final quote amount.
- If line items exist, the total should be the sum of all included line items unless an explicit stored final total overrides it by design.

- Make sure currency formatting is consistent everywhere.

- The quote creation logic
- The AI quote generation logic
- The manual quote builder logic

- The rendering logic for “Total” and “Accept Quote” CTA

- Any serialization/deserialization issue that turns undefined/null into 0

Implementation requirements:
- Use one canonical total field for sent quotes.
- Persist the total at the moment the quote is finalized/sent.
- On the customer quote page:
  - Prefer the persisted final total
  - If missing, derive it from line items
