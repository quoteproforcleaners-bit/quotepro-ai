  strategyProfile,
  escalationStage,
  messageType,
  customerContext
})

Where:
- strategyProfile = selected Sales Strategy
- escalationStage = 1,2,3,4
- messageType = QUOTE_FOLLOWUP | REACTIVATION | REBOOK | UPSELL | REVIEW | REFERRAL
- customerContext includes:
   firstName
   quoteTotal
   serviceType
   lastServiceDate
   homeSize (if available)

AI should:

- Escalate firmness based on stage
