## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

When you first open QuotePro, you can create an account using:

- **Email and password** - Enter your email, choose a password, and tap "Create Account."
- **Google Sign-In** - Tap "Continue with Google" to sign in with your Google account.
- **Apple Sign-In** - Tap "Continue with Apple" to use your Apple ID (available on iPhone).

You can also choose your preferred language (English or Spanish) right from the login screen.

### Onboarding

After creating your account, you'll go through a quick two-step setup:

1. **Business Profile** - Enter your business name and your name. This information appears on your quotes and communications.
2. **Pricing Setup** - Set your hourly rate and minimum charge. These are the foundation of your quote calculations. Don't worry, you can always change these later.

Once onboarding is complete, you'll land on the Home Dashboard.

## Home Dashboard

The Home Dashboard is your command center. It greets you by name and gives you an instant snapshot of your business.

### What You'll See

- **AI Command Bar** - A text input at the top where you can type natural language commands like "Create a new quote" or "Follow up with customers." Quick action chips below the bar offer common actions with one tap.
- **Today's Focus Card** - Shows how many follow-ups are due, the value of at-risk quotes, and your oldest outstanding quote. Tap "Follow Up Now" to jump to the Follow-Up Queue.
- **Follow-Up Streak** - Tracks how many consecutive days you've followed up with customers. Building a streak encourages consistent engagement and helps you close more deals.
- **Recent Quotes** - A list of your latest quotes with customer name, service type, price, and status. Tap any quote to see its full details.
- **Opportunities Summary** - Shows how many dormant customers and expired quotes you could win back, along with estimated recoverable revenue.
- **Weekly Recap Link** - Tap to see your performance summary for the current week.

### Quick Actions

From the Home screen, you can quickly:

- **New Quote** - Start creating a quote immediately.
- **Follow Up Quotes** - Jump to quotes that need attention.
- **Draft a Reply** - Open the AI assistant to help craft messages.

## Creating Quotes

The quote calculator is the heart of QuotePro. It walks you through four steps to create an accurate, professional cleaning quote.

### Step 1: Customer Information

- Select an existing customer from your list, or create a new one right here.
- Enter the customer's first name, last name, phone number, email, and address.
- The customer will be saved to your customer list automatically.

### Step 2: Home Details

Enter the property information that determines the quote price:

- **Square Footage** - The total size of the home.
- **Bedrooms** - Number of bedrooms (0-10).
- **Full Bathrooms** - Number of full baths (1-10).
- **Half Bathrooms** - Number of half baths (0-5).
- **Home Type** - House, Apartment, or Townhome.
- **Number of People** - How many people live in the home.
- **Current Cleanliness** - A slider from 1-10 rating the home's current condition. Lower scores mean more work and a higher quote.
- **Pet Information** - Type of pet (None, Cat, Dog, or Multiple) and whether they shed heavily. Pets with heavy shedding increase the quote.
- **Number of Kitchens** - For larger homes with multiple kitchens.

### Step 3: Service Add-Ons

Toggle on any additional services the customer wants. Each add-on has a set price that gets added to the base quote:

- **Inside Fridge** - Deep clean the refrigerator interior.
- **Inside Oven** - Deep clean the oven interior.
- **Inside Cabinets** - Clean cabinet interiors.
- **Interior Windows** - Clean all interior windows.
- **Blinds Detail** - Detailed cleaning of all blinds.
- **Baseboards Detail** - Detailed baseboard cleaning.
- **Laundry Fold Only** - Fold laundry (no washing).
- **Dishes** - Wash and put away dishes.
- **Organization/Tidy** - General organization and tidying.

You also choose the **cleaning frequency** on this screen:

- **One-time** - A single cleaning visit.
- **Weekly** - Every week (highest discount).
- **Bi-weekly** - Every two weeks.
- **Monthly** - Once a month.
- **Quarterly** - Every three months.

Recurring frequencies automatically apply a discount to encourage ongoing service.

### Step 4: Quote Preview

This is where everything comes together. You'll see three pricing options:

- **Good** - The basic cleaning package at the lowest price.
- **Better** - A mid-tier package with additional services.
- **Best** - The premium package with the most comprehensive cleaning.

Each option shows the service name, what's included (the scope of work), and the price. Tap an option to select it.

#### Sending the Quote

**Free plan users** get manual email and SMS drafts:
- **Email Draft** - A pre-written email you can copy and paste into your email app. Includes the customer name, pricing options, and your booking link.
- **SMS Draft** - A shorter text message version you can copy or open directly in your Messages app.

**QuotePro AI subscribers** get additional power:
- **AI-Written Email** - Tap "AI Write Email" to have AI generate a personalized, professional email based on the quote details. You can edit it, regenerate it, copy it, or send it directly from the app.
- **AI-Written SMS** - Same as above but for text messages. The AI crafts a concise, personalized message.
- **Direct Sending** - Send the email or text directly through the app without leaving QuotePro. Emails are sent via SendGrid and texts via Twilio.

After selecting an option and reviewing/sending drafts, tap **"Save Quote"** to save it to your quotes list.

### After Saving a Quote

Once saved, you can find the quote in the Quotes tab. From the Quote Detail screen, you can:

- **Change the status** - Mark it as Draft, Sent, Accepted, Declined, or Expired.
- **Copy a Payment Link** - If you've connected Stripe, generate a link customers can use to pay online (includes a 3% platform fee).
- **Export as PDF** - Create a branded PDF of the quote that you can share with the customer. The PDF includes your company logo, contact info, pricing options, and terms.
- **Generate AI Messages** - Create follow-up messages, thank you notes, or other communications related to the quote.
- **Delete the Quote** - Remove it from your system if no longer needed.

## Managing Customers

The Customers tab is your built-in CRM for managing all your client relationships.

### Customer List

- **Search** - Use the search bar at the top to find customers by name.
- **Filter by Status** - Filter between All, Leads, Active, and Inactive customers.
- **Add Customer** - Tap the "+" button to add a new customer manually.

### Customer Details

Tap any customer to see their full profile:

- **Contact Information** - Name, email, phone number, and address. Tap any field to edit it.
- **Status** - Lead, Active, or Inactive. Change it with a tap.
- **Notes** - Add private notes about the customer (preferences, special instructions, access codes, etc.). Tap "Add Note" to create one.
- **Tags** - Add custom tags to organize and categorize customers (e.g., "VIP," "Monthly," "Referred"). Tap "Add Tag" to create one.
- **Quotes** - See all quotes associated with this customer.
- **Jobs** - See all jobs scheduled or completed for this customer.
- **Communications** - View the history of all emails, texts, and phone calls with this customer.

### Adding a New Customer

When creating a new customer, enter:
- First name and last name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Address

Customers can also be created automatically when you build a new quote.

## Scheduling & Managing Jobs

The Jobs tab helps you manage your cleaning schedule and track job progress.

### Job List

- **Filter by Status** - View All, Scheduled, In Progress, Completed, or Cancelled jobs.
- **Add Job** - Tap the "+" button to create a new job.

### Creating a New Job

When scheduling a new job, you'll enter:

- **Customer** - Search for and select an existing customer, or create a new one.
- **Job Type** - Choose from Standard, Deep Clean, Move In/Out, Post Construction, or Airbnb Turnover.
- **Date & Time** - Pick when the job is scheduled to start.
- **Estimated Duration** - How long you expect the job to take (in hours).
- **Address** - Where the job takes place (defaults to the customer's address).
- **Total Amount** - Optionally set the price for this job.
- **Internal Notes** - Any private notes for your reference.
- **Recurrence** - Set the job to repeat on a schedule:
  - **One-Time** - A single job.
  - **Weekly** - Repeats every week.
  - **Biweekly** - Repeats every two weeks.
  - **Monthly** - Repeats once a month.

### Job Details

From the job detail screen, you can:

- **Update Status** - Move the job through its lifecycle: Scheduled, In Progress, Completed, or Cancelled.
- **Checklist** - Add task items to create a cleaning checklist. Check items off as you complete them during the job.
- **Before/After Photos** - Take or select photos to document the property before and after cleaning. Add captions to describe each photo. Great for showing customers the quality of your work.
- **Recurring Settings** - View and manage the recurrence schedule. When a recurring job is marked complete, the next occurrence is automatically created.
- **Mark Complete** - When the job is finished, mark it complete. If it's a recurring job, the next job is automatically scheduled.

### Google Calendar Sync

If you've connected Google Calendar in Settings, jobs automatically sync to your calendar when they're scheduled or updated. This keeps your personal calendar and QuotePro in sync.

## Growth Tasks Queue

The Tasks Queue is a unified list of all growth-related tasks generated by QuotePro's growth engine.

### Task Types

QuotePro generates 7 types of growth tasks:

1. **Quote Follow-Up** - Remind you to follow up on outstanding quotes.
2. **Abandoned Recovery** - Reach out to customers who started but didn't complete the booking process.
3. **Rebook Nudge** - Suggest reaching out to customers who are due for their next cleaning.
4. **Review Request** - Ask satisfied customers to leave a review.
5. **Referral Ask** - Ask happy customers for referrals.
6. **Upsell Deep Clean** - Suggest offering a deep clean to regular customers.
7. **Reactivation** - Reach out to dormant customers.

### Priority System

Each task gets a priority score from 0-100:
- **High Priority** (red badge) - Urgent tasks that should be done today.
- **Medium Priority** (yellow badge) - Important but not urgent.
- **Low Priority** (gray badge) - Can be done when you have time.

### Escalation Dots

Tasks show escalation dots indicating which stage they're in (up to 4 stages). As tasks age without action, they escalate to more urgent messaging.

### Actions

For each task, you can:
- **Complete** - Mark the task as done.
- **Skip** - Skip this task.
- **Escalate** - Move to the next urgency stage.

### Filters

Filter tasks by type using the filter buttons at the top of the screen.

A master toggle that turns all automations on or off. When Marketing Mode is ON, QuotePro automatically creates growth tasks based on your settings.

## Sales Strategy

Customize how QuotePro communicates with your customers.

### Sales Profiles

Choose from 4 communication styles that affect how AI generates messages:

1. **Professional** - Polished, respectful, and straight to the point.
   *Example: "Hi [Name], thank you for requesting a cleaning estimate. I have availability this week and would be happy to schedule your service at your convenience."*

2. **Friendly** - Warm, casual, like texting a neighbor.
   *Example: "Hey [Name]! Hope you're having a great week. Just wanted to check in about your cleaning quote - I'd love to help keep your place looking amazing."*

3. **Premium** - Luxury feel, white-glove, exclusive.
   *Example: "Good afternoon [Name]. As part of our commitment to exceptional service, I'd like to personally extend priority scheduling for your home."*

4. **Urgent** - Direct, time-sensitive, creates action.
   *Example: "[Name], quick heads up - your quote expires tomorrow and I only have 2 openings left this week. Reply now to lock in your rate!"*

### Escalation Engine

When enabled, messages automatically increase in urgency with each follow-up:

- **Stage 1: Soft Touch** - A gentle reminder. *"Just a friendly reminder about your cleaning quote. No rush - let us know if you have any questions!"*
- **Stage 2: Value Add** - Highlights benefits. *"Did you know? Our deep clean includes baseboards, inside appliances, and window sills at no extra charge."*
- **Stage 3: Urgency** - Creates time pressure. *"Heads up - our schedule is filling fast for next week. We'd hate for you to miss out on your preferred time."*
- **Stage 4: Final** - Last chance messaging. *"Last chance! Your custom quote expires tomorrow. After that, we'll need to re-estimate based on current rates."*

### AI Message Preview

Preview how your messages will sound with your chosen profile and escalation settings before they're sent to real customers.

- **Sender Title** - Your title (e.g., "Owner").
- **Booking Link** - A link where customers can book (e.g., your Calendly link).

Auto-added to outgoing messages:

### Growth & Automations

Quick links to:
- **Automations Hub** - Configure marketing mode, workflows, and guardrails.

## Language Settings

QuotePro supports English and Spanish with a unique dual-language system.

### App Language

This controls the language of all menus, buttons, labels, and text throughout the app. Change it in Settings > Language Preferences > App Language.

### Customer Communication Language

This controls the language of messages sent to your customers, including:
- AI-generated email drafts
- AI-generated SMS drafts
- Quote descriptions
- Any AI-generated content

Change it in Settings > Language Preferences > Customer Messages Language.

### Why Two Settings?

This is designed for bilingual cleaning business owners. For example:
- A Spanish-speaking owner who serves English-speaking customers can set the app to Spanish and communications to English.
- An English-speaking owner with Spanish-speaking customers can set the app to English and communications to Spanish.
- Or set both to the same language if that's what you prefer.

## Tips for Success

1. **Build your follow-up streak** - Consistent follow-ups are the key to closing more quotes. Try to follow up every day to build and maintain your streak.

2. **Use Good/Better/Best pricing** - Offering three tiers gives customers a choice and often leads them to pick the middle option, increasing your average quote value.

3. **Take before/after photos** - Document your work on every job. This builds trust with customers and gives you content to share.

4. **Set up recurring jobs** - When a customer accepts a recurring frequency, set up the job as recurring. QuotePro automatically schedules the next occurrence when you complete a job.

5. **Connect Google Calendar** - Keep your personal calendar in sync with your QuotePro jobs so you never double-book.

6. **Connect Stripe** - Make it easy for customers to pay by including a payment link in your quotes.

7. **Customize your branding** - Add your logo, set your sender name and title, and configure your email/SMS signatures. Professional branding builds trust.

8. **Review your Weekly Recap** - Check your performance every week and set goals to keep improving.

9. **Use the Growth Dashboard** - Let QuotePro identify opportunities you might miss, from upsells to dormant customers.

10. **Configure your Sales Strategy** - Pick a communication style that matches your brand. Enable the escalation engine for automated urgency progression on follow-ups.
